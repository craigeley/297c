Optional Equipment

We have two required audio assignments that will use technology available through Penn State MediaTech, specifically the Zoom H1 Audio Recorder. MediaTech and the Library Commons have ample audio technology resources which we will be working with throughout the semester. Still, students wishing to incorporate audio into additional projects, including the final one, might find it convenient to have their own recorder. The H1 is usually available for around $80 on Amazon. This is completely optional, and will not affect your grade in any way.
